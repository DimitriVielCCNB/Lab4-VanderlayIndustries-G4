How to Create an Account - Vanderlay

Welcome to Vanderlay! Follow the steps below to create your account:

  1. Visit the Vanderlay Sign Up page: https://www.vanderlay.com/signup
  2. Fill in the required information, including your name, email address, and a secure password.
  3. Choose a unique username for your account.
  4. Agree to the terms and conditions, and click the "Create Account" button.
  5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your registered email.
  6. Once verified, you can now sign in to your Vanderlay account using your credentials.

If you encounter any issues during the account creation process, please contact our support team at support@vanderlay.com.

How to Sign In - Vanderlay

Welcome to Vanderlay! Follow the steps below to sign in to your account:

  1. Visit the Vanderlay Sign In page: https://www.vanderlay.com/signin
  2. Enter your username and password in the designated fields.
  3. Click the "Sign In" button to access your account.

If you encounter any issues during the sign-in process, please contact our support team at support@vanderlay.com.
